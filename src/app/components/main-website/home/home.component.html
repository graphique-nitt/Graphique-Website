About Us

Graphique, the official graphic designing club of NIT Trichy, aims to spread the message of Design Thinking. Its activities are primarily centered around creating artworks for the institute's festivals - Festember and Pragyan. Graphique also takes up campus development initiatives to help everyone learn Graphic Design through workshops and Bootcamp by leading graphic designers. Mentored by our alumnus, the club members also take up various industrial and real-life problem statements and regularly engage with the global community to collaboratively solve the problem statements. Graphique operates on multiple profiles such as Digital Art, Graphic Design, UI/UX, 3D Art, Motion Graphics, etc. Graphique got established on 22nd October 2019. Graphique currently has 49 active members: 4 core members, 22 managers and 23 coordinators.

Our Journey

August 2012
Founded as Team

A group of people interested in creative skills such as graphic design and digital art created the NITT design team to spread and establish a design community in NIT Trichy.

March 2013
Started working for Festember & Pragyan

NITT Design Team has historically undertaken various fest works, majorly Festember and Pragyan dating back to 2013 and other regular design projects.

May 2014
Graphic Design Lectures

The goal of these lectures were to equip learners with a set of transferable formal and conceptual tools for “making and communicating” in the field of graphic design.

July 2015
Contribution for Student Council

Started to help the student council of NIT Trichy with posters design and branding. We started to do various campus development activities and initiatives.

January 2016
Participation in External Competitions

We started to participate in various design competions and workshops such as Strate Design Contest, March of Robots, Kuruksastra Design Events, Adobe 99U Conference, etc.

September 2018
We conducted our first ever public workshop

We created an incredible opportunity for the freshers to garner insight into how to channel the artist inside of them and will equip them with the skills required to bring your creative and artistic vision to life.

October 2019
Officially Recognised as Club by Student Council

We are given club status and were officially made as the design club of National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli on 22nd October, 2019.

May 2020
Graphique Bootcamp

We started to conducted a Bootcamp which teaches basics of Graphic designing for a fresher, and other essential design skills for their life at college. We also offered a Digital Certificate of completion after their successful completion.

December 2020
Chroma

We started to conduct Chroma, an online Art Contest exclusively for the freshers of NIT Trichy, in which participants will be challenged to overcome their artistic limits and expand their creative horizons by utilising a set of unique prompts and keywords!

September 2021
6K+ Followers o Instagram

We are overwhelmed by the love we get from our Instagram followers. Much gratitude to our followers for being such great support. Much obliged for the consistent remarking and enjoying it fully.

Febraury 2022
First Video Editing Workshop

For the first time in NIT Trichy, we conducted an exciting workshop on Video Editing, Colour Correction, 3D Art and Motion Graphics. An excellent opportunity for students to add new skills to their skillset!
